Help Center

Find answers to common questions and get support

How do I get started with OneTapCampus?

Simply click the "Start Now" button on our homepage and follow the registration process. You'll need your university email address to create an account.

Is OneTapCampus free to use?

Yes! OneTapCampus is completely free for all students. We believe education should be accessible to everyone.

Which universities are supported?

We're currently expanding our support to universities across Bangladesh and internationally. Contact us to request support for your university.

How secure is my data?

We take data security seriously. All data is encrypted, and we follow industry best practices to protect your information.

Can I use OneTapCampus on my mobile device?

Absolutely! OneTapCampus is fully responsive and works perfectly on all devices including smartphones and tablets.
